#+TITLE: Flash On Debian


* Upating It

Occasionally you will get an error in Iceweasel telling you that Flash is out
of date. In that situation, simply execute this command:

#+BEGIN_EXAMPLE
    $ sudo update-flashplugin-nonfree --install
#+END_EXAMPLE
