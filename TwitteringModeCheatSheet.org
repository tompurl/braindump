#+TITLE: TwitteringModeCheatSheet

- "Regular" retweet
  - =C-u C-c C-m=
- Post a tweet
  - =u=
- Open a user's timeline
  - Navigate to a tweet from that user and then press =v=
- Open a timeline from a list of names
  - =V=
- Favorite a tweet
  - =M-x twittering-favorite=
- Shorten a URL
  - =C-c C-k=
- Search for a term
  - =C-c C-q=
- Reply to a tweet
  - =C-m=
