#+TITLE: Revealjs Tips

* Printing Presentations

** General

To print a presentation you have to do the following:

- Host it with a web server
- Open it with Chrome

** URL's

- http://web-hosted-url/?print-pdf
- http://web-hosted-url/?print-pdf&showNotes=true

** Creating Handouts With 4 Slides Per Page

*** Generating The PDF

First, when printing, select "Print using system dialog". Then make
the following choices:

General:
- PDF

Page Setup:
- Pages per side: 4
- Top to bottom, left to right
- orientation: landscape

*** Printing With Evince

After opening the PDF choose your real printer. Then make the
following choices:

Page Setup:
- two sided: long edge
- Orientation: landscape
